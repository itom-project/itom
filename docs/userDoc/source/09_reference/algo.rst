Algorithms, Widgets and Filters
*********************************

.. currentmodule:: itom

The algorithm-plugins are containers for **filters** and **widgets**. They do not have an own instance. Filters 
are called by the **filter(...)**-Methods. To get an online help use **filterHelp(...)**. 
Widgets are used with **ui**-dialogs. To get an online help use **widgetHelp(...)**.
